Context

PeerPrep is a multi-service interview prep platform. Question flows needed a dedicated backend that could filter, randomize, and serve problems at scale.

What I shipped

  • Question Service microservice with 15 REST endpoints (Node.js, Express, TypeScript)
  • MongoDB aggregation pipelines for randomized selection and filtered queries
  • Python pipeline ingesting the TACO dataset into 300+ curated interview problems
